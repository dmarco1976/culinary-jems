# Culinary JEMs — Menu Data

## The Sliders

All sliders are gourmet-sized with premium, chef-selected ingredients. Served on artisan brioche buns.

---

### 1. Irwin M. Classic
- **Protein**: Beef (grass-fed)
- **Description**: Grass-fed beef patty, grilled onions, pickle, stone ground mustard
- **Story**: The OG. Named after Fletch's most classic alias, this is the slider everyone starts with. Simple, perfect, no-nonsense. If you're new, start here.
- **Key selling points**: Grass-fed beef, classic flavors done right, crowd favorite
- **Pairs with**: Any craft lager or pilsner
- **Featured**: Yes (homepage)
- **Photo**: ig-sliders-rack.jpg

### 2. I'm A Shepherd
- **Protein**: Beef (grass-fed)
- **Description**: Grass-fed beef patty, aged cheddar, boursin, provolone
- **Story**: Triple cheese because commitment matters. The name's a Fletch deep cut for the real fans. Aged cheddar + boursin + provolone = cheese lover's dream.
- **Key selling points**: THREE artisan cheeses, rich and indulgent
- **Pairs with**: Amber ale or brown ale
- **Featured**: No
- **Photo**: slider-closeup.jpg

### 3. The Fat Sam
- **Protein**: Beef (grass-fed)
- **Description**: Four Peaks Kilt Lifter braised beef, tobacco onions, roasted garlic aioli
- **Story**: The showstopper. Beef braised in local Four Peaks Kilt Lifter Scotch Ale until fall-apart tender, topped with crispy tobacco onions and house-made roasted garlic aioli. This is the one people talk about.
- **Key selling points**: Local craft beer braise, tobacco onions, most Instagram-worthy, "the one people come back for"
- **Pairs with**: Scotch ale (obviously), stout, or porter
- **Featured**: Yes (homepage)
- **Photo**: ig-goldwater.jpg

### 4. Chief Karlen
- **Protein**: Pork (tenderloin)
- **Description**: Pork tenderloin, grilled onions, havarti, arugula, lemon aioli
- **Story**: The sleeper hit. Pork tenderloin isn't common on food trucks — that's the point. Havarti melts perfectly, arugula adds peppery bite, lemon aioli ties it together. Pork fans know.
- **Key selling points**: Unique protein choice, balanced flavors, "the one regulars order"
- **Pairs with**: Hefeweizen or wheat beer
- **Featured**: No
- **Photo**: slider-closeup.jpg

### 5. Arnold Babar
- **Protein**: Chicken (buttermilk fried)
- **Description**: Buttermilk fried chicken breast, pickle, spicy mayo
- **Story**: Crispy, juicy, spicy. Our fried chicken slider holds its own against any Nashville hot chicken in the Valley. Buttermilk brine + perfect fry + spicy mayo = *chef's kiss*.
- **Key selling points**: Buttermilk fried, crispy perfection, spicy kick, comfort food elevated
- **Pairs with**: IPA or pale ale
- **Featured**: Yes (homepage)
- **Photo**: ig-food-kitsune.jpg

### 6. Dr. Rosenrose
- **Protein**: Turkey (organic meatball)
- **Description**: Organic turkey meatball, marinara, arugula, pecorino romano
- **Story**: The lighter side of gourmet. Italian-inspired turkey meatball with house marinara, peppery arugula, and shaved pecorino romano. For when you want something different but still 100% JEMs quality.
- **Key selling points**: Lighter option, Italian-inspired, organic turkey, unique on a food truck
- **Pairs with**: Chianti or a light red ale
- **Featured**: No
- **Photo**: ig-food-carousel.jpg
