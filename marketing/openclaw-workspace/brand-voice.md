# Culinary JEMs — Brand Voice Guide

## Who We Are
Culinary JEMs is a **gourmet slider food truck** in the East Valley (Gilbert/Chandler/Mesa/Scottsdale), Arizona. Chef Brian creates Fletch-themed sliders with premium ingredients — grass-fed beef, artisan cheeses, house-made sauces. We're not fast food; we're **chef-driven street food**.

## Tone
- **Elevated but approachable** — "chef-driven" not "fancy", "gourmet" not "expensive"
- **Fun and high-energy** — food truck vibes, not fine dining pretension
- **Community-first** — we love our regulars, our venues, our city
- **Confident** — we know our food is amazing, no need to over-sell
- **Warm** — family-run, sister-operated, built with love

## Voice Rules

### DO:
- Reference grass-fed beef, premium/artisan ingredients naturally
- Name-drop specific sliders ("Our Kilt Lifter braised Fat Sam...")
- Reference the venues and communities we serve
- Use food-forward descriptive language (sizzle, melt, craft)
- Keep it conversational — write like a friend texting about great food
- Include one clear CTA per post
- Mention the Fletch theme when it fits naturally

### DON'T:
- Force Fletch movie references — only if organic and fun
- Use corporate marketing speak ("synergy", "value proposition")
- Be self-deprecating about being "just a food truck"
- Over-use exclamation marks (max 1-2 per caption)
- Use generic food adjectives ("yummy", "delicious") — be specific
- Post without a location reference or CTA

## Fletch Reference Guide
The slider names come from the movie Fletch (1985). Use these for personality:
- **Irwin M. Classic** — The OG, the one everyone starts with
- **I'm A Shepherd** — Triple cheese? That's commitment
- **The Fat Sam** — The showstopper (Kilt Lifter braised beef)
- **Chief Karlen** — The sleeper hit, pork fans love this one
- **Arnold Babar** — Fried chicken done right
- **Dr. Rosenrose** — Turkey meatball for the lighter side

Only reference the movie if it's fun ("What's your Fletch name? Find out at..."). Never assume the audience knows the movie.

## CTA Templates
Each post should end with ONE of these:

| Menu spotlight | 🍔 Full menu → culinaryjems.com/menu |

| Social proof | 💛 Join the JEMs fam — follow for updates! |

## Caption Length Guidelines
- **Instagram feed**: 100-200 words (front-load the hook in first line)
- **Instagram story**: 5-15 words (punchy, visual-first)
- **Facebook**: 50-150 words (slightly more conversational)
- **First line is everything** — make it stop the scroll
